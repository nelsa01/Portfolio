Who is Nelsa?

Nelsa Ishimwe is a fourth-year student in Computer Science at African Leadership University. She was born in Rwanda and currently lives at Kimironkp. She's pursuing Computer science as she has a love for coding and would like to improve her creativity while helping design people's websites. She's an outgoing person and prefers to spend her free time with her friends.

Projects

Menya 250

This is an app that was developed to raise attention to Rwanda's beautiful features to locals and internationals through a word puzzle game. It was developed using ReactJs and Django.

Sustainable Solution

This is a solution that was developed to help unprivileged students stand a chance to get sponsorship where schools and students would use our app to get in touch with people who are willing to sponsor them. It was developed using ReactJs and Nodejs.

Poet Portal

This was an app developed to help young poets in Rwanda to come together and be able to create poems using our app. This app was developed using ReactJs and Django.

Online Ticket System

This is a command-line system that was developed to help people in Rwanda travelling to rural areas book their tickets online instead of having to go and stand long queues. This system was developed using python.

Expense Tracker

This app was developed to help people to track their expenditures, and be able to budget their incomes. It was developed using flutter.

iheart

This is an AI that was developed to help people easily detect if they have any heart disease. It was developed using python and other model training packages.
